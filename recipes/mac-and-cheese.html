Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description of dish:

Mac and Cheese is a cheesy pasta dish.

Ingredients:

  • Box of Macaroni Noodles
  • Cheese Powder
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Seasonings to Taste

Steps:

  1. Boil macaroni noodles to desired doneness.
  2. Strain pasta water, reserving 1/2 cup.
  3. Add cheese power to pasta water.
  4. Mix in butter and milk, reducing until thickened.
  5. Combine noodles and finished cheese sauce.
