Hot Dog Recipe

Description of dish:

A hot dog is a meat conglomerate sausage on a bun, typically with condiments.

Ingredients:

  • Hot Dog Weiner
  • Hot Dog Bun
  • Optional: Condiments of Choice

Steps:

  1. Boil or fry your hot dog to your liking.
  2. Prepare bun for dogging.
  3. Place cooked hot dog in the bun.
  4. Optional: add your favorite condiments.
