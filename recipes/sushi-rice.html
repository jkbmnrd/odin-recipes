Sushi Rice Recipe

Description of dish:

Sushi Rice is a short-grained white rice dish with oil, rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.

Ingredients:

  • Short-Grained White Rice
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Neutral Flavored-Oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Using a rice cooker, prepare your rice.
  2. Let rice cool completely.
  3. While the rice cools, begin preparing the rice sauce.
  4. Mix oil, rice vinegar, sugar, and salt over heat.
  5. Combine cooled rice and rice sauce.
